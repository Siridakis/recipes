Simple recipe for guacamole

Ingredients:
 - 1 Avocado
 - 1 Purple Onion (you can use a regular one too)
 - 1 Tomato
 - 1 Lemon
 - salt and pepper as you see fit

 Slice the onion and tomato into small cubes. Smash the avocado with the juice
 from the lemon. Mix the other ingredients. Leave it in the refrigerator for at
 least an hour. Enjoy.
